Latest updates

FEBRUARY 2023

Launch of NEW LibreTactile Website

We are thrilled to announce the launch of our new website featuring improved navigation and functionality, making it easier for visitors to access information about our mission, projects, and how to get involved.

DECEMBER 2022

Tomat Project Update: Experimental phase completed!

This milestone marks a significant step forward in our research, and we're grateful for the hard work and dedication of our team. We'll now be analyzing the results and look forward to sharing our findings in the near future.

MARCH 14TH 2022

Tomat Project Update: Restart of project after COVID-19!

We are excited to announce that our research project is resuming after a temporary pause due to the COVID pandemic. With safety measures in place, we are eager to continue our work and move forward towards our goals.
